Why Sleep Matters

Good sleep improves memory, mood, immunity and energy. Adults should aim for 7 to 8 hours of sleep daily.

Sleep Score Guide

Excellent Sleep = You successfully achieved your sleep goal. This indicates a healthy sleep routine that can improve energy, concentration, mood, and overall well-being.

Almost Reached Your Goal = Your sleep duration is slightly below the target. Although your sleep is fairly good, getting a little more rest can help improve recovery and daily performance.

Need More Rest = Your sleep is significantly below the recommended goal. Insufficient sleep may lead to tiredness, reduced focus, stress, and lower productivity during the day.

Sleep Tips

Sleep at same time daily.
Avoid mobile before bed.
Keep room dark and cool.
Get 7 to 8 hours sleep.
Avoid caffeine at night.
Use comfortable pillow.
Stretch before sleep.
Listen to calm music.
No heavy meals late night.
Wake up same time daily.
